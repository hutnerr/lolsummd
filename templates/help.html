Overview

lolsummd allows you to combine the champion mastery data of multiple League of Legends accounts into one big summary.

You can add up to 10 accounts at a time from any region.

Instructions

On the home page, add up to 10 accounts by entering their username and tag (e.g., SummonerName#NA1 -> SummonerName and NA1).

After adding the accounts, click "Get Combined Mastery" to retrieve the combined champion mastery data.

You can remove individual accounts by clicking the "Remove" button next to each account or clear all accounts by clicking the "Clear" button.
