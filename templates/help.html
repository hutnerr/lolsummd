Help

Everything you need to get started

Overview

lolsummd lets you combine the champion mastery data of multiple League of Legends accounts into one unified summary.

You can add up to 10 accounts at a time from any region.

✦

Instructions

On the home page, add accounts by entering a username and tag — for example, SummonerName#NA1 becomes SummonerName and NA1.

Once you've added your accounts, click Get Combined Mastery to retrieve the aggregated data.

Remove individual accounts with the Remove button, or wipe the whole list with Clear All.
